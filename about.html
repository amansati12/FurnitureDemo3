Our story

Brand story

Born from the belief that luxury should feel lived-in.

Aurelia Living began as a small design studio creating custom pieces for architects and private residences. Today, we blend artisan construction, modern silhouettes, and tactile materials into furniture that feels composed from the first glance and comfortable for years.

Mission

Create enduring furniture that elevates daily life with craft, restraint, and comfort.

Vision

Shape homes where design intelligence and emotional ease live together.
